About Me

Hi! I'm a second-year Ph.D. student in Software Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University. I am advised by Dr. Eunsuk Kang and Dr. Fraser Brown. In the spring of 2023, I received my Bachelor's degree in Aerospace Engineering from Iowa State University.

Research Interests

I am interested in the application of formal methods to software systems. Particularly, I am interested in the application of formal modelling and verification to cyber-physical systems and security systems.
